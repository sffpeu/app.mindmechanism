Founding Authority

Mind Mechanism is not a product that was designed and then tested. It is a theory that was observed first — in real rooms, with real learners, across real cultures — and then formalised into the architecture this document describes.

The methodology was developed over decades of corporate language delivery in Germany: banking, insurance, big industry, technology. The approach built there was communicative, immersive, and deliberately engineered so that engagement was not optional — not through mandate but through design. The room was constructed so that the path of least resistance was involvement. That approach was delivered across cultures, across genders, across the specific frictions of institutional hierarchy and personal history. The results are evidenced. The misses, when they came, were not failures of the method — they were the places where cultural expectation or personal history ran deeper than a single engagement could reach.

What that work produced — at considerable personal cost, and without adequate recognition or return — was thirty years of observed acquisition compressed into a 482-node taxonomy of interior states, a named pedagogical principle, and a clinical protocol that replicates the results reliably. The IP is the record of that work. The licensing model is how it finally receives its value.

This is not a platform built to generate revenue. It is a platform built because the work demanded a structure adequate to its ambition. The revenue is a consequence of that — not its purpose.

The Sovereign Assessment Architecture

The architecture is one shell. Three distinct portals. A single framework, accessed through differentiated entry points calibrated to the specific needs of the corporate client, the student, and the consumer. Each portal is sovereign. Each is sealed.

The Mechanism

Every client — corporate or individual — is issued a blockchain key attached to a wallet. That key is theirs. It locks them to their section of the platform and to nothing else. Their data does not travel. Their results do not surface. Their progress is held in an architecture that no employer, no teacher, and no platform administrator can penetrate — because the system was not built to permit that penetration. Not as a policy. As a structural impossibility.

The Philosophical Position

The current model of institutional assessment operates on an assumption that has never been adequately examined: that external judgement improves performance. The evidence does not support this. What the evidence shows, consistently, is the opposite. The approach of external judgement does not raise performance. It raises anxiety. And raised anxiety, in the runup to any assessment, suppresses the precise cognitive and linguistic capacities the assessment was designed to measure. The system produces the conditions that guarantee it will underperform.

This is not an administrative failure. It is structural. It was built in an era when the power asymmetry between institution and individual was so total that the costs to the individual were simply not considered as costs. That era is over. The awareness of consequence is here. The social responsibility is here. The evidence of harm is here. And a framework that continues to operate on the old architecture is not merely inefficient — it is, in the precise sense of the word, an abuse of the authority the institution has been given over the person in its care.

The Mind Mechanism is built on a different premise. The learner is not a subject of assessment. They are the author of it.

The Seven License Types

Seven distinct instruments govern the full licensing structure. Each addresses a different buyer relationship and different commercial logic. All underlying IP remains with Sean Fortune in every instrument type.

License 1
Consumer EULA
Who: Individual subscribers (Open, Standard, Sovereign).

Logic: Non-transferable personal use. Subscription is a license, not a purchase.

Key restrictions: No reproduction or redistribution of taxonomy content; no commercial use; no reverse engineering; no scraping.
License 2
Practitioner License
Who: Coaches, therapists, facilitators deploying MM professionally with paying clients.

Logic: Practitioners generate revenue using MM IP. This captures that value without competing with them.

Key restrictions: Clients receive no independent taxonomy rights; mandatory attribution in client-facing materials.
License 3
Academic Research License
Who: Universities, research institutions, doctoral researchers.

Logic: Below-market pricing in exchange for citation and findings sharing. Academic validation compounds the commercial value of the IP over time.

Key restrictions: Mandatory citation; no commercial application without separate agreement.
License 4
Corporate Master License Agreement
Who: Organisations accessing MM for employee or client use.

Logic: Seat-based or organisation-wide access. Premium pricing justified by specificity and originality of IP.

Key restrictions: Named organisation only; no sub-licensing; German governing law; annual renewal.
License 5
Pack License
Who: Corporate clients purchasing pre-built or bespoke content Packs.

Logic: Packs are discrete IP objects. The runtime license creates perpetual recurring revenue from each Pack deployed.

Key restrictions: Named organisation only; no redistribution; Pack license terminates with MLA unless separately agreed.
License 6
White Label Agreement
Who: Organisations deploying a fully branded MM instance under their own name.

Logic: Highest-value single instrument. Branding rights licensed, not assigned. IP and authorship remain with Sean Fortune.

Key restrictions: IP acknowledgement clause mandatory; sector exclusivity available at premium; 5-year maximum term.
License 7
Third-Party IP License
Who: Publishers, application developers, content producers using the taxonomy or ESL in their own products.

Logic: The taxonomy has value beyond the MM application. Royalty-bearing with annual accounting and audit right.

Floor: €10,000/year minimum against 3–5% royalty on attributable revenue.
