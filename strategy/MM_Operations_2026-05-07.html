Three-Portal Architecture

One shell. One taxonomy. Three sovereign environments. The mandala and the node are the constant. The Pack fills them. The interface reads differently in each space because the material is different — but the architecture is identical.

Portal One
Corporate
Hedera HBAR
Feel: Dense and precise. A professional tool that reads like one.
Content: Banking language. Governance vocabulary. Decision-making frameworks. Sector-specific Pack builds.
Key: Corporate key + personal wallet. Boss sees engagement only. Never results.
Revenue: MLA fee + bespoke Pack development + perpetual runtime license tail.
Chain rationale: Google, IBM, Deutsche Telekom on governing council. Procurement answer before the question is asked.
Timeline: Conversations Q4 2026. Hedera live Q2–Q3 2027.
Portal Two — First to Launch
Education
Algorand ALGO
Feel: Structured and progressive. A curriculum with a spine.
Content: Node-sequenced curriculum. Emotive Prerequisite series as Pack skeleton. Builds on itself.
Key: Institutional key + personal wallet. Teacher sees engagement only. Never results.
Revenue: Academic license + Pack library subscription + curriculum adoption fees.
Chain rationale: Fractions of a cent per transaction. Carbon neutral. Fast finality. Cost absorbed by MM entirely.
Timeline: Portal live September 2026. Blockchain Q1 2027.
Portal Three
Consumer
Algorand ALGO
Feel: Less but feels like more. Every element earns its place. An arrival, not a syllabus.
Content: Curated Packs. Warm but uncompromising in precision. Entry point into the full ecosystem.
Key: Personal wallet key. No third-party visibility. Nothing.
Revenue: Consumer subscription €99–199/year. Gateway to practitioner tier and physical materials.
Chain rationale: Same as education. Cost negligible. Sovereignty guaranteed.
Timeline: Live now at theOneLeggedPoet. Blockchain layer Q1 2027.

The Cross-Portal Content Economy

Every piece of content produced has a natural home across all three spaces with minor adaptation. A node populated for a banking Pack can be stripped back for the consumer interface and restructured for the education curriculum without rebuilding from scratch. A consumer who moves into the education space finds the same house with different furniture. The taxonomy is the address. The familiarity is the product.

The Commercial Philosophy — Non-Negotiable

No middle man
Self-publishing. Direct sales.
Full sovereignty over the product, the customer relationship, and the revenue. No third party withholding mailing details or user data.
No bank loan
Strategic invoicing only.
No dilution. No covenants. No investor timeline that is not Sean's. The first Pack fee funds the next build phase. Each stream carries the next.
No perishable stock
The taxonomy does not expire.
Stock held is stock appreciating. Earlier titles compound in value as the catalogue grows. This is not a trend-dependent publishing model.
